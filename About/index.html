Triveni Museum

Discover the rich heritage and cultural significance of Triveni Museum through a captivating journey of art, history, and innovation. Immerse yourself in the stories of the past and present, beautifully preserved within our walls.

History of the Museum

2012

Planning and conceptualization of the Triveni Museum began to preserve the archaeological and cultural heritage of Ujjain.

2014

Construction of the museum building started near the Triveni Ghat, along the banks of the Shipra River.

2016

The Triveni Museum was officially inaugurated, opening its doors to the public with exhibits showcasing ancient artifacts and cultural relics of Ujjain.

2017

The museum began organizing cultural events, workshops, and temporary exhibitions to engage visitors and promote the region's heritage.

2020

The museum expanded its collection to include more archaeological findings and introduced digital displays to enhance the visitor experience.

2023

Renovations were undertaken to upgrade the museum's infrastructure, ensuring better preservation of artifacts and improving accessibility for visitors.
